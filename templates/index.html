SLGS OBU Presidential Election

Welcome to the Sierra Leone Grammar School Old Boys Union Presidential Election

How to Vote:
  1. Receive your unique 8-digit Voter ID from the election committee
  2. Go to the voting page and enter your Voter ID
  3. Vote for candidates in all 12 positions (President, VP, Secretary, etc.)
  4. Submit your votes - you can only vote once per election!
Important: Your Voter ID is unique and can only be used once. Keep it secure and use it to cast your votes.
